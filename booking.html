Booking

You can book directly with the hotel using this link which provides a 5% discount for our guests. Please note: Bed & Breakfast comes as standard for each booking however you can add half-board or all inclusive if you wish (Once you've selected the dates and room type, you have to go to the next stage to add these).

You can also book the hotel through The Wedding Travel Company (who we booked the wedding through). They are a registered travel agency and can offer interest free monthly payments and low deposits. If you want more information then please get in touch with Katie or Ash.
