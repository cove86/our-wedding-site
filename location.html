Location

Our wedding will take place at The Golden Coast Beach Hotel on Thursday 6th June 2024. We will be staying at the hotel for 10 days from 2nd June 2024 but guests can book as many or as little days as they wish and do not have to stay at this hotel if they don't want to. The hotel is ideally situated with a beach literally a few meters from the hotel and a marina where you can book boat trips! The hotel is a 10 minute drive from Protaras and a 15 mintue drive from Aiya Napa, both of which have a wide selection of shops, restaurants, bars and other fun things to do!
